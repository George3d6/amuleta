Amuleta [github]

A traditional, coffeebreak roguelike

Amuleta is a traditional, coffeebreak roguelike, in which the player must guide an adventurer through a perilous dungeon in the search for the Amulet of Light.

The dungeon is ten levels deep, and you must fight the Guardian of the Amulet, which resides at the bottom level.

Being a coffeebreak roguelike, its stat system is quite simplified — the only attributes a player has are body, mind, and luck.

The general theme is boring old high fantasy. Steel swords and wands of frost, and that kind of stuff. Also goblins, bats, and Mystic Wizards.
